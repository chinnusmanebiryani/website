Our Story

Chinnu's Mane Biryani is delighted to serve namma Bengaluru some delicious Nati style Biryani. The Biryani that we prepare is unique in its own way and never fails to tickle your taste buds.

The unique spices that goes into our Biryani gives an authentic taste. Every ingredient that goes into our dishes are handpicked and made sure it's of good quality. Our motive is to serve our customers some delicious hygienic food without compromising on quality and quantity.
